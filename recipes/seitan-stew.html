Seitan Stew

A hearty and comfortable recipe, that is perfect for the winter!

Seitan is king here, while an abundance of onions, carrots and leeks make up its delicious reign.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • White Wine
  • Tomato Sauce
  • 1 onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 4 carrots
  • 1/2 a leek
  • Around 15 cherry tomatoes
  • 1 big block of seitan (or 300g)
  • Available herbs and spices

Preperation

  1. Cut the onion and 2 cloves of garlic and saut√© them in a bit of Olive Oil for 5 minutes in a pan
  2. Cut the carrots in thin half rounds and add them to the onions. Mix them and add a pinch of salt an Black Pepper
  3. Cut the leek into half rounds and add them to the pan. Cover the mixture with white wine and let it simmer for 10 minutes
  4. Meanwhile, cut the Seitan into little cubes and the cherry tomatoes into halves and add them to the Pan
  5. Mince the rest of the garlic cloves and add them to Pan. Mix for some seconds.
  6. Add 200ml of tomato sauce, mix for a while and add some water until it covers the whole mixture, or until it reaches a preferred thickness
  7. Add some chives, rosemary and thyme, a tablespoon of olive oil and honey and let it cook in low heat for 10 minutes
